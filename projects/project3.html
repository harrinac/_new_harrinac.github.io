Project Scope
Deliverable Trained Sales Volume Forecasting Model
Machine Learning Task Time Series Analysis
Target Variable Monthly Sales Forecast
Control Metric RMSE $11330 over a forecasted 6 month horizon

This project showcases a time series visualization and sales volume prediction workflow. The challenge is to predict 6 months sales figure using time series analysis of historical sales data. The data used is a real-world anonymised dataset from e-commerce retailers.

Techniques are shown for visualizing time series data, for decomposing into trend and seasonality components and for testing for non-stationarity. Methods are presented for transforming a non-stationary series into a stationary series for analysis. This notebook provides a comparison of time-series sales forecasting methods of increasing complexity from simple moving averages to SARIMAX forecasting methods.

The quality of the forecasts is measured using the RMSE of 6 months forecasted sales data with actual/real sales values.
